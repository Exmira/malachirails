About Me

Hi, I’m Malachi, a Ruby on Rails developer, educator, and storyteller passionate about turning complex concepts into actionable, hands-on learning experiences. My journey into the Rails world started years ago when I wanted to create a platform to solve a problem I deeply cared about.


With no formal coding background, I dove into tutorials and forums, eventually falling in love with the power of Rails to bring ideas to life. That experience taught me the value of learning by doing—and it’s the foundation of everything I teach today.


Now, I run a YouTube channel where over 5000+ subscribers from around the globe tune in to master Rails development. My content focuses on real-world projects, like building e-commerce platforms, integrating Hotwire, or deploying to production without breaking a sweat. My mission? To make coding approachable, exciting, and empowering for developers of all levels.
